## Principles
- holism: seeing a system as a whole rather than reducing it to its components.
- Interconnectedness: All parts of a system are connected, sometimes in non-obvious ways. Ignoring these connections often leads to side effects and unintended
- Feedback loops: is a circular cause and effect chain where actions influence future states
- Emergence: behavior that arise from interactions among components. Can't be predicted from the behavior of individual parts
- causality and delay
