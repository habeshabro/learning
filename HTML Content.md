## Paragraph
the p tag is usually under used but it has two primary uses when compared to loose text:
- Semantic: It tells the browser, reader and whatever is trying to "understand" the document that it is a paragraph
- Styling: By default, p is a block-level element that has a line break before and after it
```html
<p>this is a paragraph</p>
```
